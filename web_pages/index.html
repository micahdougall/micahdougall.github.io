The Motivation

Tom's trip to the UK last summer was his highlight of the year. But, it was truly tragic that he had to return his Stand-up Paddle Board for something he didn't have to try and stand up on. He decided enough was enough, so back on the fitness bandwagon he went.../

The Journey

Fuelled by little more than a surplus of testosterone and a blind sense of optimism, Tom has stopped at nothing in search of his weight-loss goal. No one else really understands his questionable techniques but he persists regardless. He will stop at nothing.

The Goal

Tom is determined to align his own self-perception with reality. This might be an ambitious goal but you can be sure his vanity will drive him to the very limits of his physical capabilities. We all wish him well.
